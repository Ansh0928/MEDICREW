MediCrew — Swarm Architecture

Main Specialists + Resident Sub-Agents — Full MiroFish pattern

👁️
What the patient sees
A side panel showing their medical team working live — residents consulting, debating, and the lead specialist correcting them. Not overwhelming because it streams progressively and collapses after each phase completes.
L0 Patient Input
Age, symptoms, context, medical history, what they've already tried
Example
"I'm 23, having lower back pain from cycling. Started 3 days ago. No medication, just been resting."
L1 Triage Agent
Assesses urgency · Identifies lead specialist · Flags any supporting consultants needed
Triage Output
Urgency: routine · Lead: Emma AI — Physiotherapy · Supporting: Alex AI — GP (secondary) · Red flags: none detected
L2 Lead Specialist Activates
Emma AI — Physiotherapy takes ownership of the consultation and activates her resident team
L3 Resident Swarm — 4 agents run in parallel
🌿
Conservative
Non-pharmacological approach. Physical therapy, heat/cold, rest, posture.
💊
Pharmacological
Medication options, OTC vs prescription, dosing, contraindications.
🔍
Investigative
Tests or assessments needed. Posture analysis, imaging criteria.
🚨
Red-flag
Urgent/serious causes to rule out. Nerve signs, fracture risk, systemic causes.
Resident outputs (streaming to patient side panel)
🌿 Conservative: "Hot pack 20 min 3×/day + gentle hip flexor stretches. Confidence 78%."
💊 Pharmacological: "Ibuprofen 400mg with food if pain >4/10. Confidence 65%."
🔍 Investigative: "Check saddle height and handlebar reach — likely postural cause. Confidence 82%."
🚨 Red-flag: "No radiculopathy signs. No bowel/bladder flags. Safe to proceed conservatively."
L4 Resident Debate
Residents respond to each other — agree, challenge, or add context
challenge 💊 Pharmacological → 🌿 Conservative: "Rest alone risks deconditioning. Early mobility is better evidence."
add context 🔍 Investigative → all: "Cycling-specific back pain usually resolves with bike fit correction. Should be first line."
agree 🌿 Conservative → 🔍 Investigative: "Agree — bike fit + gentle movement. Meds only if no change day 4."
L5 Lead Specialist Rectification
Emma AI reviews the debate, corrects any wrong residents, adds clinical judgment, produces team position
Emma's rectification
"Pharmacological resident is correct that rest alone isn't ideal, but medication at day 3 is premature for a young patient with no prior treatment. Correcting to: active recovery + bike fit + heat. Medication is appropriate if unresolved after 5 days. Team confidence: 81%."
L6 MDT Cross-Consult (if flagged)
Supporting specialists give brief input — 1-2 sentences each
Alex AI — GP weighs in
"Agree with physio approach. If no improvement in 7 days, consider GP assessment to rule out underlying cause."
L7 Synthesis — Final Output
🌿 Active recovery: gentle movement + hip flexor stretches daily
81% confident
🚴 Check bike saddle height and handlebar reach — likely root cause
82% confident
🔥 Hot pack 20 min × 3 daily for first 3 days
78% confident
💊 If no improvement by day 5 → ibuprofen 400mg with food
conditional
Still no improvement after 7 days? A physio can assess in person.
