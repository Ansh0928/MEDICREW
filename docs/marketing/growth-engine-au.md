# AU-First Growth Engine Playbook

## Content Loops

1. Publish one symptom-intent resource cluster update per week (`/resources`).
2. Map each cluster to one short-form founder video and one FAQ snippet for landing reuse.
3. Link every content piece to one clear CTA: start consultation.

## Partner Pilot Motion

- Target early partners: GP clinics, allied health, community health groups.
- Offer a 4-week pilot with:
  - onboarding support,
  - KPI dashboard review,
  - patient safety and escalation training.
- Pilot success criteria:
  - consultation completion uplift,
  - return-visit uplift,
  - no compliance incidents.

## Product Growth Hooks Shipped

- Saved consultation summaries in portal flow
- Share summary action in `SynthesisCard`
- Follow-up reminder narrative in consult/landing copy

## Weekly Operating Cadence

- Monday: KPI review + experiment selection
- Wednesday: content + distribution publish
- Friday: partner funnel review + experiment results log
